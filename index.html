I received my Master degree in Physiology from Hangzhou Normal University in June 2024, and my Bachelor degree in Biopharmaceutical from Changshu Institute of Technology in June 2021. During my Master's studies, my research focused on exploring the neural mechanisms of language function in patients with brain injuries. Specifically, I investigated the white matter network connections underlying semantic processing in stroke patients, as well as the diagnosis, prognosis, and epidemiological characteristics of disorders of consciousness in patients with severe brain injuries. The findings from my research have been published in journals such as Brain Communication and NeuroImage.
